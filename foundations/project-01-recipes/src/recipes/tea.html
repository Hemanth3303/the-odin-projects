Tea


Photo by Matheus Bardemaker on Unsplash

Description

Tea is very good. Good to dunk cream biscuit and nom nom.

Ingredients

  • water
  • milk powder
  • sugar
  • tea leaf powder

Steps

  1. boil water in utensil.
  2. put sugar in once water heats up.
  3. wait till boiling.
  4. put in tea leaf powder.
  5. cover let sit for a while.
  6. strain and pour into mug with milk powder
  7. keep mixing two three times
  8. serve and drink
  9. dunk cream biscuit and eat(optional).
