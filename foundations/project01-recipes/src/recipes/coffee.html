Coffee


Photo by Ante Samarzija on Unsplash

Description

Coffee is very good. Makes sleep go away.

Ingredients

  • water
  • milk powder
  • sugar
  • instant coffee powder

Steps

  1. boil water in utensil.
  2. put sugar in once water heats up.
  3. wait till boiling.
  4. put coffee powder and milk powder into a mug.
  5. pour boiling water.
  6. keep mixing two three times.
  7. serve and drink.
  8. good bye to sleep.
