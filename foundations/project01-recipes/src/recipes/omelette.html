Omelette


Photo by Anirudh on Unsplash

Description

omelette good, tasty yum.

Ingredients

  • egg
  • onion
  • chilli
  • salt
  • oil

Steps

  1. break egg and put in bowl.
  2. wisk.
  3. mix salt, chopped onion and cut chilli.
  4. mix well, don't leave salt chunks like mom.
  5. coat pan with oil.
  6. pour egg mix and move around till flat like dosa.
  7. wait till cooked, then flip.
  8. make sure both sides cooked, (we don't want salmonella in this house), then serve, eat nom nom.
